"let's code"

Welcome to PTSC, where innovation meets coding! Our club fosters a community of tech enthusiasts passionate about programming, development, and real-world problem-solving.

Our vision is to become a leading student-run platform where future tech leaders emerge by exploring new technologies, collaborating on impactful projects, and preparing for global tech opportunities.

Our Journey

2014: PTSC was founded to create a coding culture within campus.
2017: PTSC organized its first national-level hackathon, attracting 200+ participants.
2019: Club members secured internships with top tech firms like Google and Amazon.
2025: PTSC is thriving with AI/ML workshops, real-world projects, and inter-college collaborations.

We regularly host coding sessions, tech talks, inter-college contests, and career-building workshops to shape future tech leaders.

Explore Topics

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the simulation of human intelligence in machines. It enables systems to perform tasks like learning, reasoning, and problem-solving, making them capable of mimicking human behavior and decision-making in dynamic environments.

Machine Learning (ML) is a subset of AI that uses statistical techniques to give computers the ability to learn from data and improve from experience without being explicitly programmed. ML powers applications like recommendation systems, fraud detection, and self-driving cars.
